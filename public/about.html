About Me

I see challenges as opportunities to innovate, and I’m driven by the vision of creating impactful startups that combine technical precision with real-world utility.

Outside of work, I’m an athlete at heart—a football goalkeeper for my college team and someone who values discipline, fitness, and self-improvement. Whether it’s on the field, in the gym, or through yoga and mindfulness, I believe in pushing boundaries and staying balanced. This same mindset fuels my personal and professional life: to grow, adapt, and constantly strive for excellence.
